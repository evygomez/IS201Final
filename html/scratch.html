What is Calisthenics?

Calisthenics refers to exercises that rely on your body weight for resistance training. It can improve strength, flexibility, and endurance.

    • L-Sit
  3. Advanced
    • Planch
    • Human Flag
    • One Arm Pull-ups
    • Front and Back Levers
